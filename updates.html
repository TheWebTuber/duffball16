Channel News & Updates

Want To Keep Up with What's New on Duffball16? This is your official hub for all the latest channel updates, announcements, and behind-the-scenes info. Bookmark this space and check back often to stay connected with everything happening on the channel.

New Series Launch: Marvel Rivals

Date: April 15, 2025 Big news for the channel - a brand-new gameplay series is here. I’m diving into the fast-paced team shooter Marvel Rivals. Expect epic matches, deep character builds, and strategic gameplay, all edited with the high quality you’ve come to expect. The first episode premieres later this week, and future parts will include viewer suggestions and community poll choices. It’s not just about winning, it’s about having fun and creating memorable moments together. Stay tuned and let me know your favorite Marvel characters you want to see in action!

New Milestone Goal: 30,000 Duffinians

Date: April 10, 2025 Thanks to your amazing support, we recently crossed 20,000 subscribers! Now, our next big goal is to reach 30,000 Duffinians. To celebrate major subscriber milestones, we host special livestream events called The Duffine Tourneys, where more fans can join, engage, and play live with me and the community. It’s a fantastic way to connect, compete, and have a blast together. You can find examples of past Tourneys on the YouTube channel. If you’re already part of the family, thank you - and if you’re new here, now’s the perfect time to jump in and be part of something awesome.

Updated Livestream Schedule: Weekend Hangouts

Date: April 5, 2025 After listening to your feedback and reviewing our stream performance, I’ve updated the livestream schedule to be even more consistent. From now on, livestreams will happen every weekend, starting around 6 or 7 PM UK time. Saturdays will usually feature scheduled gameplay, while Sundays are kept flexible for surprise sessions, special guests, or chill streams. Make sure to hit the bell icon on YouTube so you’re always notified when we go live. Come hang out, chat with me in real-time, and be part of the fun!
